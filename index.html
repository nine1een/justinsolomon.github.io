Mission Statement

"To provide unsurpassed janitorial services to all of our customers. Our goal is to achieve total customer satisfaction by consistently delivering top quality services and by building enduring relationships with all of our customers."

Vision Statement

Our vision is to achieve excellence in adhering to our three primary objectives:

The highest level of customer satisfaction through consistent quality performance.

Management support, encouragement and performance that will enable clients, employees and communities to achieve first-class service in their field.

Honest and ethical business practices with clients, employees and the communities we serve.

Who we are

Christian Service Company was founded in Jefferson City, MO in 1980 by Steve & Karen Griggs. The original focus of the company was strictly on commercial cleaning maintenance. Today, we stand alone as the premier full-service cleaning company in Mid-Missouri. We provide residential and commercial cleaning, along with our specialized floor cleaning. Christian Service Company has a strong reputation for providing high quality cleaning at affordable prices.

We promise

Whether it is once a month, or every night, we ensure that everything is done right. From toilets to windows, we do it all. We take pride in our employees and commitment to meeting your needs.
