The Party

We went out and had some fun.

Sometime in March my friend Tyler invited me out to his job's annual work party. Well it wasn't really much of a work party. It was more inviting to outsiders than work parties usually are. Drinks were passed around but I was more focused on enjoying the sight of people in what I would consider a vunerable moment. I've always been quite shy so it's easy for me to notice the same trait in others. There were people who seemed incredibly shy but with music flooding the areas around them they submitted to their own body's desire to move.

Sundry #1

The first collection of micsellaneous photos.

When I first began taking photos I was incredibly attracted to capturing the simplest of things. Many people would often ask me what I saw in something that they thought to be unworthy of captured and my answer would always be that I just liked it. Sometimes I don't think of creating art, I simply want to show I like. Art is defined by those who view it and thats what this is — things I thought to be artsy or just things I liked.

The People in My Life

Family, Friends, Lover

The people who usually have the biggest impact on your life are those who are the closest to you. It's not only family but the friends you keep that eventually help mold you into the person you become. It's not these people directly that alter your path, it's more like the things they expose you to — emotions, personalities, lessons. If it were not for these people I would not be the person I now am.
